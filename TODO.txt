Standards
---------
Ease of use is more important than mnemonics

c - create
d - delete
n - next
p - previous
x - delete

<c-f> - up
<c-p> - down

Organise object keys alphabetically
a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z

- neo-tree: g and z normal mode shortcuts
- neo-tree: remove exisiting shortcuts
- neo-tree: preview scroll mappings
- terminal: better editing
- Autocommand to strip trailing whitespace (%s/\s\+$//e)
